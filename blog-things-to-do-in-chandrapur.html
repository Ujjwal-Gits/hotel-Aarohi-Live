Top 5 Things to Do in Chandrapur, Rautahat, Nepal

May 17, 2026 · by Hotel Aarohi

Chandrapur, nestled in the heart of Rautahat district in Nepal's Terai region, is a vibrant municipality brimming with cultural heritage, natural beauty, and warm hospitality. Often referred to locally as Chapur, this growing town is an ideal destination for travelers seeking an authentic Nepalese experience. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a food lover, or simply looking for a peaceful retreat, Chandrapur has something special waiting for you.

1. Explore the Ancient Temples of Chandrapur

Chandrapur is steeped in religious and historical significance. The municipality is home to several ancient Hindu temples that date back centuries. These temples are living monuments to the rich cultural tapestry of the Terai. Walking through the temple grounds, you can admire traditional Nepalese architecture, intricate wood carvings, and a serene atmosphere. The best time to visit is during local festivals when the temples come alive with colorful celebrations.

After a day of exploration, return to the comfort of Hotel Aarohi, the best hotel in Chandrapur, and relax in our premium rooms.

2. Savor Authentic Thakali Cuisine in Chapur

No visit to Chandrapur is complete without indulging in the region's famous cuisine. The star of the local food scene is the Thakali set — a traditional platter of dal, rice, seasonal vegetables, pickles, and meat curry. Hotel Aarohi is proud to serve the best Thakali in Chandrapur — an authentic taste of heritage crafted with premium local ingredients. Beyond Thakali, our restaurant menu features Mo:Mo, Sekuwa, Paneer Tikka, and continental dishes.

3. Take a Nature Walk Along the Countryside

The flat, verdant landscape of Rautahat is perfect for peaceful morning or evening walks. The agricultural fields surrounding Chandrapur paint a stunning picture, especially during the rice planting and harvest seasons. Birdwatchers will find plenty to admire — the region is home to a variety of wetland and grassland bird species. For guests at Hotel Aarohi, our staff can recommend the best walking routes around Chapur.

4. Visit the Local Markets of Rautahat

Chandrapur's local bazaars are a treasure trove of authentic Nepalese goods. From handwoven textiles and brass utensils to fresh spices and seasonal fruits, the weekly haat bazaar draws vendors from across Rautahat. It's the perfect place to pick up souvenirs while supporting local artisans. Located in the heart of the municipality, Hotel Aarohi is within easy walking distance of all major market areas.

5. Attend a Local Festival or Cultural Event

Chandrapur and Rautahat celebrate a rich calendar of festivals including Dashain, Tihar, Chhath Puja, and Holi. Visitors who time their trip to coincide with a major festival will be rewarded with unforgettable memories. If you are planning to visit during festival season, book your room at Hotel Aarohi early — as the best hotel in Rautahat, our rooms fill up quickly during peak periods.
